IBM RXN for Chemistry

IBM RXN for Chemistry is an AI-driven platform that enables chemists to automate and optimize synthetic chemistry reactions. By utilizing machine learning algorithms, the platform predicts reaction outcomes and suggests synthetic routes, helping researchers save time and resources in the lab. Users can input their desired reaction conditions, and RXN provides insights based on historical reaction data. This tool is particularly beneficial for pharmaceutical and materials science applications, where efficient synthesis is crucial.
