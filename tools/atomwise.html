Atomwise

Atomwise employs artificial intelligence and deep learning to enhance drug discovery through structure-based modeling. Their platform analyzes molecular interactions and predicts the binding affinities of potential drug candidates, enabling researchers to identify promising compounds more rapidly. Atomwise's technology has applications in various therapeutic areas, including oncology and neurodegenerative diseases. By leveraging vast datasets and advanced algorithms, Atomwise streamlines the drug discovery process, making it a valuable partner for pharmaceutical companies and research institutions.
