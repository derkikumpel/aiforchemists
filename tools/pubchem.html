PubChem

PubChem is a comprehensive, freely accessible database that provides detailed information on chemical molecules and their biological activities. Managed by the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI), PubChem includes data on compound structures, properties, and biological activities against various assays. Researchers use PubChem as an essential tool for drug discovery, as it facilitates the exploration of chemical space and helps identify promising candidates for further investigation. The extensive dataset and advanced search features make it a vital resource for chemists and biologists alike.
