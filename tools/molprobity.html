About MolProbity

MolProbity is a powerful software tool widely used in the field of structural biology for validating and visualizing macromolecular structures such as proteins and nucleic acids. It provides researchers with critical insights into the quality of their structural models by performing detailed assessments of stereochemistry, geometry, and atomic interactions. The tool evaluates various aspects, including bond angles, torsion angles, and van der Waals contacts, helping users identify potential structural errors or deviations from ideal geometries. MolProbity also offers interactive graphical interfaces that allow users to easily visualize structural features and problematic regions, facilitating informed decision-making during model refinement. Additionally, it incorporates comprehensive databases and scoring systems to benchmark structural quality against established standards. Whether used in the initial stages of model building or in the final validation of structures prior to publication, MolProbity is an essential resource for researchers seeking to ensure the accuracy and reliability of their macromolecular models, ultimately contributing to advancements in fields such as drug design and molecular biology.
