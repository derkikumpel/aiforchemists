ChemSpider

ChemSpider is a free chemical structure database that provides access to over 67 million chemical compounds, offering researchers a wealth of information for drug discovery and cheminformatics. Users can search for chemical structures, properties, and spectra, as well as access links to supporting data and literature. ChemSpider is an essential resource for scientists looking to explore chemical data, conduct research, and gain insights into molecular interactions, all while supporting collaboration across the scientific community.
