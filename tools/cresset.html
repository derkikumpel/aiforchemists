Cresset

Cresset provides innovative molecular modeling and analysis tools that aid researchers in drug discovery. Their software allows users to visualize molecular interactions, perform structure-based design, and analyze compound libraries. By integrating various modeling techniques with user-friendly interfaces, Cresset enhances the ability of researchers to identify and optimize lead candidates. The platform supports diverse applications, including virtual screening and ADMET predictions, making it a valuable asset in the pharmaceutical industry.
