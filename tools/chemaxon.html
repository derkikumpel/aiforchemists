ChemAxon

ChemAxon offers a suite of cheminformatics and bioinformatics tools that support various stages of drug discovery. Their software solutions enable researchers to visualize chemical structures, perform data analysis, and conduct virtual screenings. With features like structure search, property prediction, and molecular modeling, ChemAxon helps streamline the drug design process, ensuring that researchers can efficiently analyze chemical data and make informed decisions in their projects.
