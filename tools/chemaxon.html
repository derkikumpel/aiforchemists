ChemAxon

ChemAxon offers a suite of cheminformatics tools that support molecular modeling, visualization, and data management. Their software solutions cater to various stages of drug discovery, from structure elucidation to compound library design. Features include predictive modeling, molecular visualization, and database management, all aimed at enhancing the efficiency of the drug discovery process. ChemAxon's tools are widely used within pharmaceutical companies and academic institutions to facilitate research and development.
