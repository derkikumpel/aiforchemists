ChEMBL

ChEMBL is a large-scale bioactivity database that provides valuable information on drug-like molecules and their biological activities. Curated from scientific literature, ChEMBL includes data on compound properties, targets, and bioactivities, making it a vital resource for researchers in drug discovery. The database supports the identification of potential drug candidates and aids in understanding structure-activity relationships. ChEMBL's user-friendly interface and robust search capabilities enable efficient data retrieval, facilitating research in pharmacology and medicinal chemistry.
