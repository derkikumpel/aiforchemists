Zinc Database

The ZINC Database is a free online resource that provides access to a vast collection of commercially available compounds suitable for virtual screening. Researchers can search for specific molecular features, download compound libraries, and utilize the database in conjunction with docking and molecular modeling studies. ZINC is widely used in the pharmaceutical industry to facilitate hit identification and optimize lead compounds, significantly enhancing the efficiency of drug discovery processes.
