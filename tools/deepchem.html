DeepChem

DeepChem is an open-source library that provides tools for applying deep learning to various aspects of drug discovery and cheminformatics. It offers a user-friendly interface to manipulate molecular data, integrate with existing machine learning frameworks, and perform tasks such as molecular property prediction, molecular generation, and virtual screening. Its extensive collection of pre-trained models and datasets makes it an invaluable resource for researchers looking to leverage AI in drug development processes.
