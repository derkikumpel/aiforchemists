Atomwise

Atomwise is an AI-driven drug discovery platform that leverages deep learning algorithms to accelerate the screening of compounds for potential therapeutic use. By analyzing molecular interactions and predicting their biological activity, Atomwise helps researchers identify promising drug candidates more efficiently than traditional methods. The platform is designed to facilitate collaboration between academic institutions and pharmaceutical companies, aiming to reduce the time and cost associated with drug development while improving the success rate of new compounds.
