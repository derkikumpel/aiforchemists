DeepChem

DeepChem is a robust open-source library designed to bring the power of deep learning to drug discovery and cheminformatics. It provides tools for molecular graph representation, machine learning algorithms, and various datasets, facilitating the development of predictive models for molecular properties, bioactivity, and other essential characteristics. Researchers can leverage DeepChem to accelerate drug discovery processes while tapping into the extensive capabilities of modern machine learning techniques.
