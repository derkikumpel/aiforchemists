ChEMBL

ChEMBL is a large-scale database that aggregates information on bioactive drug-like small molecules and their biological activities. Managed by the European Bioinformatics Institute, it provides chemists and biologists with access to a wealth of data relevant for drug discovery, including compound structures, target information, and bioactivity data. Researchers can use ChEMBL to identify potential drug candidates, understand molecular interactions, and explore structure-activity relationships. The database is continuously updated and includes tools for data retrieval and analysis, making it essential for anyone involved in pharmaceutical research.
