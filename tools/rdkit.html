RDKit

RDKit is a powerful open-source cheminformatics toolkit that provides extensive functionality for the manipulation, analysis, and visualization of chemical data. It is widely used in both academic and industrial settings for tasks such as molecular descriptor calculation, substructure searching, and cheminformatics data mining. RDKit's Python-based interface allows for easy integration into data analysis workflows, enabling researchers to develop custom applications for drug discovery. With its robust documentation and active community, RDKit is an essential tool for computational chemists and cheminformatics researchers looking to enhance their research capabilities.
